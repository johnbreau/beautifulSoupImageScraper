East Midlands Roleplayers are a group (rather than an organised club) of like minded gamers. We have no committee, no 'membership' (other than optional registration on our website) and there are no fees or costs (although any donations towards the cost of hosting the website are gratefully received)

Many of our members run and play in tabletop and live action roleplaying games around the East Midlands.

Our website provides excellent facilities to meet local like minded folk and discuss, plan and play games. You can get started by registering here.

If you find our site useful please consier a small donation to help with our costs
